Project Overview

AB Mini Cabs is a modern taxi booking platform serving the Thornton Heath and surrounding areas. The client needed a reliable, user-friendly website where customers could instantly book rides, get fare estimates, and manage their bookings.

The solution involves a streamlined booking flow, integration with dispatch systems, and a responsive design that works perfectly for users booking on the go via their mobile phones.

Key Features

  • Instant Fare Quotes: Real-time pricing based on distance and vehicle type.
  • Online Booking Engine: Seamless 3-step booking process.
  • Meet & Greet Service: Special handling for airport transfers.
  • Mobile Optimized: Designed for "thumb-friendly" navigation on smartphones.
